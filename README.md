# 🎯 Zero Trace - Hacker vs Defender Duel

**Zero Trace** is a **high-intensity, fast-paced 1v1 cyber duel game** where players take on the roles of either a Hacker 🕶️ or a Defender 🛡️. Each match lasts **only 30 seconds**, but in that short time, strategy, skill, and quick thinking determine the winner. The game blends thrilling gameplay with real-world cybersecurity concepts, making it both fun and educational.  

---

## 🔥 Core Gameplay

- **Choose Your Role:**  
  - **Hacker:** Attempt to breach your opponent’s network using clever tactics, stealth, and speed.  
  - **Defender:** Protect your systems, detect intrusions, and counteract attacks before time runs out.  

- **30-Second Duels:**  
  Every match is a fast-paced battle of wits. You have just half a minute to outmaneuver your opponent, making each decision critical and each second count.  

- **Asymmetric Combat:**  
  Hackers and Defenders have completely different abilities and strategies, creating intense, unpredictable matches where experience and clever thinking are key.  

- **Skill Development:**  
  While playing, you’re not just competing—you’re learning. The game introduces real-world cybersecurity concepts like:  
  - Network defense and penetration tactics  
  - Threat detection and mitigation strategies  
  - Tactical decision-making under pressure  
  - Understanding attack and defense dynamics  

- **Replay and Strategy:**  
  Because matches are short, players are encouraged to experiment with different tactics. Every duel is a chance to learn, refine strategies, and anticipate the opponent’s moves.
